    Frequently Asked Questions

    Below are some of the commonly asked questions along with their most accurate answers.

    What is your mission?

    Our mission is to provide quality affordable, and painless dental care, ensuring healthy and confident smiles for all our patients.

    Where are you located?

    We are located at Royal Square, 6th Floor, along Ngong Road. We also have a second branch to serve you better.

    Do you accept insurance cards?

    Yes, we accept insurance cards, including SHA, and other recognized providers.

    What dental services do you offer?

    We offer a wide range of dental services, including general check-ups, teeth cleaning, teeth whitening, braces installation, dental fillings, extractions, and implants consultations.

    What are your operating hours?

    Our clinic is open Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

    Do you offer discounts for students?

    Yes! We offer a special student package for only Ksh. 500 only for a limited number of services, covering a dental check-up and consultation.
